# Velyon Brand Kit

This Brand Kit outlines the core visual identity assets for Velyon, a strategic AI consultancy. The visual identity reflects a premium, executive, and technologically advanced positioning, leveraging an "Executive Dark Mode" aesthetic.

## 1. Color Palette: Executive Dark Mode

The Velyon color palette is designed to evoke trust, authority, and cutting-edge innovation.

### Primary Colors (Foundational)
*   **Deep Obsidian (Background)**
    *   **Hex:** `#0A0A0A`
    *   **RGB:** `10, 10, 10`
    *   **Usage:** Primary app and website background. Represents the deep tech foundation.
*   **Graphite (Surface)**
    *   **Hex:** `#171717`
    *   **RGB:** `23, 23, 23`
    *   **Usage:** Card backgrounds, modals, elevated surfaces. Creates depth against Obsidian.
*   **Off-White (Primary Text)**
    *   **Hex:** `#F5F5F5`
    *   **RGB:** `245, 245, 245`
    *   **Usage:** High-contrast body text and headings. Avoids the eye strain of pure `#FFFFFF` on dark backgrounds.

### Secondary Colors (Accents & UI)
*   **Electric Azure (Primary Accent/Tech)**
    *   **Hex:** `#3B82F6` (Tailwind Blue 500)
    *   **RGB:** `59, 130, 246`
    *   **Usage:** Primary buttons, active states, key data points, AI-specific features.
*   **ROI Gold (Secondary Accent/Trust)**
    *   **Hex:** `#EAB308` (Tailwind Yellow 500)
    *   **RGB:** `234, 179, 8`
    *   **Usage:** Highlighting guarantees (e.g., "The 2X ROI Guarantee"), premium features, specific metrics of success.
*   **Neutral Grey (Secondary Text/Borders)**
    *   **Hex:** `#A3A3A3`
    *   **RGB:** `163, 163, 163`
    *   **Usage:** Subtitles, placeholder text, subtle borders.

## 2. Typography

We use a modern, highly legible sans-serif stack that bridges the gap between B2B professionalism and modern SaaS aesthetics.

### Primary Typeface: Plus Jakarta Sans
*   **Role:** Primary font for all headings (H1-H6), navigation, and call-to-actions.
*   **Weights:** Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600), Bold (700)
*   **Styling:** Clean geometry provides a tech-forward look while remaining approachable.

### Secondary Typeface: Inter
*   **Role:** Body copy, data tables, microcopy, UI elements.
*   **Weights:** Light (300), Regular (400)
*   **Styling:** Designed for high legibility on digital screens, perfect for dense information and dashboards.

## 3. Brand Assets & Logo Configuration

### Logo Variations
1.  **Primary Logo (Light):** For use on dark backgrounds (Obsidian/Graphite). Clean, unembellished logomark + wordmark in Off-White `#F5F5F5` with an Electric Azure `#3B82F6` accent on the 'V'.
2.  **Monochrome Logo:** Solid white `#FFFFFF` for complex backgrounds.
3.  **Logomark Only:** For use in confined spaces (avatars, favicons, app icons).

### Imagery & Art Direction
*   **Photorealistic Assets:** Human-centric consulting settings mixed with abstract, high-end 3D technological elements (e.g., neural networks depicted elegantly, not cheaply).
*   **Infographics:** Clean line art, isometric geometry. Data must look structured and professional, avoiding playful or "cartoonish" styles.
*   **Avoid:** 
    *   Cheap stock photos of "AI robots."
    *   Overused pink/purple "cyberpunk" gradients. 
    *   Playful or cartoon-style illustrations.
